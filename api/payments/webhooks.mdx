---
title: "Payment webhooks"
description: "How ProxyJam receives payment confirmation from providers."
---

Payment providers notify ProxyJam when a payment is completed by sending a webhook to:

```
POST https://api.proxyjam.com/webhooks/payments/{provider_id}
```

<Note>
  This endpoint is called by payment providers, not by your application. You do not need to call it directly.
</Note>

## What happens after a webhook

When a payment webhook is received:

1. ProxyJam verifies the payload signature.
2. The associated wallet balance is credited (for top-up intents).
3. For orders in `pending_payment` status, the order is transitioned to `active` and proxies are provisioned.
4. A notification is sent to the user.

## Idempotency

Webhooks are processed exactly once using an inbox deduplication pattern. Duplicate webhooks from the provider are safely ignored.

## Delivery failures

If your proxies are not provisioned within a few minutes of payment, contact support with your `payment_intent_id` or order ID.
